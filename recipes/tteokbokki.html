Rose Tteokbokki

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 lb tteokbokki tteok
  • 4 strips bacon sliced into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 stalk green onion sliced
  • 3-4 sheets fish cake sliced into bite sized pieces
  • 12 mini sausages

Rose Sauce

  • 1 cup anchovy stock
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoon gochujang
  • 1 tablespoon gochugaru
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar packed

Garnish

  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon green onion sliced
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds
  • 3 boiled eggs soft or hardboiled
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Soak the tteokbokki. Soak the tteokbokki in warm tap water while you prepare the sauce.
  2. Cook the bacon. In a large deep skillet, cook the bacon until crispy. Remove from pan and set aside.
  3. Saute the garlic. In the same skillet, add 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, minced garlic and sliced green onions. Saute on medium heat for about 30 seconds or until lightly browned and fragrant.
  4. Add the sauce ingredients. Pour in the anchovy stock, heavy cream, milk, gochujang, gochugaru, soy sauce and brown sugar. Mix to combine. Bring the sauce to a boil over medium heat for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add tteokbokki. Once the sauce is boiling, add the drained tteokbokki and cook for 4-5 minutes or until soft. Stir occasionally so they don’t stick to the bottom.
  6. Add fish cake and sausages. Next, add the fish cakes, sausages. Continue to cook until the sauce thickens, about 5-10 more minutes depending on how thick you want the sauce.
  7. Add bacon and sesame oil. Turn off the heat and add the bacon and sesame oil and mix to combine.
  8. Serve. To serve, scoop the rose tteokbokki into a small bowl, sprinkle on toasted sesame seeds, sliced boiled eggs, green onion and parmesan cheese. Enjoy!
